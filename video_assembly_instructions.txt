VIDEO ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS
==============================

1. Image file: scene_image.png
2. Dialogue audio: None
3. Music audio: placeholder_music.mp3

To create the final video:
- Use a video editor like DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, or even online tools
- Import the image and set it as the video track
- Import the dialogue audio and sync it with the image
- Add the music as background audio (lower volume)
- Export as MP4
